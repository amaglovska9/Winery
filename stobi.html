STOBY WINERY

Stobi winery is located only 80 km from the capital of Skopje, in the heart of Macedonia, in the Tikves wine region – the biggest and the most important wine region which stretches on 13.000 ha, along the same latitude as Rioja and Ribera del Duero. As the Mediterranean climate from the south collides with the continental climate from the north,it creates an area most remarkable for grape growing and wine production on the entire Balkan Peninsula. We chose to place our winery in the locality of Gradsko, in vicinity of the ancient city of Stobi, where the two rivers Erigon (Crna river) and Axios (Vardar) cross their path. It is also where we cultivate all of our vineyards.
