WINES OF MACEDONIA

Wines of Macedonia (WoM) is an organization that unifies
the work of Macedonian wine producers, committed to promoting
the quality and image of Macedonian wine throughout the world.
The Association is established in April 2010 as a NGO to represent
common interests of its members as well as:
• provide strategic support to the Macedonian wine sector
including developing the wine and viticulture industry in Republic of Macedonia
• increase export of both bottled and bulk wines
• build an umbrella recognition of Macedonian wines
on the regional and international markets
• advocate in front of Government of Republic of Macedonia
and other relevant institutions.

OUR MISSION

To build and promote the image of Macedonia
as a wine country and increase the awareness
and recognition of Macedonian wines on international
markets, worldwide, in order to enable greater export
opportunities and enhance income generation for grape
growers and wineries.

OUR VISION

To facilitate Macedonia’s recognition as one
of the leading wine production countries in
South East Europe, through our collective efforts.

BENEFITS FOR THE MEMBERS

Wines of Macedonia provides full assistance and
facilitation in strategic export program development
and participation of its members to most relevant
wine promotions and trade fairs around the world.
Through WoM members are exposed to pertinent
international wine critics, wine challenges,
inbound trade missions, international knowledge and education.
WoM also provides representation and lobbying in front of
domestic and international institutions and organizations.
